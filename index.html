Features

User Management

Users can register, log in, and manage their profiles.Different user roles (e.g., patients, doctors) with specific permissions.

Online booking

Patients can conveniently book vaccination appointments online, selecting the vaccine type, preferred date, and specific time.

Vaccine Inventory

Management of vaccine stock, including adding, updating, deleting and tracking vaccine availability.

Dose Scheduling

Users can schedule doses and receive reminders for upcoming doses, ensuring timely and efficient immunization.

Data Encryption

Protect all sensitive data, like patient records, by securely encrypting it during both transit and storage.

Review and Feedback

Users can submit detailed reviews and feedback on their vaccination experience, including staff and service quality.
